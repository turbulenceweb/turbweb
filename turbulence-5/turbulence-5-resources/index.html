Turbulence 5 Resources

Turbulence exists on a very low budget. The magazine is free and we don’t carry advertising. As a result, we need as much help as we can get distributing the magazine. If you’d be interested in helping us do so, here are a few resources that might be useful.

We have an A4 poster advertising the magazine. If your local bookshop or cafe carries Turbulence, ask them to put up one of these posters. We’ve got one in colour here and one in black and white (to follow soon).

If you edit a zine, magazine or newspaper and would be able to offer us some free advertising, you can find an A6 full colour advert here. We can make up ads with alternative specifications at short notice, so get in touch (editors@turbulence.org.uk) if this format isn’t appropriate for your publication.

If you don’t yet have copies of the magazine, or we’re not able to get hard copies to your corner of the world, Turbulence 5 is available in full online. You could help us promote the online edition by distributing flyers at events, or leaving them in cafes, bookshops or info tables at conferences, gigs or meetings. You can find a black and white A5 flyer for photocopying here.

Below you can find a collection of web-banners to use to link to Turbulence from your blog or website. Other generic Turbulence web-banners can be found here.
